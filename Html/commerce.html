About Institute

  • Established in the year 2001, New LJCC is a leading self-finance institute imparting education in the field of commerce in Ahmadabad. Since its inception, it has been striving to produce students with excellent knowledge in the fields of commerce, business and finance so that they are equipped to take-up and successfully complete post-graduate courses in the related fields.

  • Besides achieving academic excellence, the institute aims at all round personality development of the students through various co-curricular activities which go a long way in building their confidence, positive attitude and discipline enabling them to make their mark and find a respectable position in the society.

Mission

  • To strive to be a leading partner in societal development by providing world class education

Vision

  • To provide innovative new age quality education system in shaping leaders of tomorrow.
