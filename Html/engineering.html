About Institute

  • LJ Institute of Engineering and Technology (LJIET) started its academics in 2007 under the aegis of Lok Jagruti Kendra (LJK) to run Under-Graduate Degree Engineering and Post-Graduate Degree Engineering Programmes. A planned, disciplined environment and beautiful green landscaping accompanied by the sweet sound of birds along with an ebullient hunt for knowledge by the young aspirants provides a unique atmosphere in the LJ Campus. The environment inspires a learner to seek the quest for knowledge and develop their skillset in any preferred areas.

  • LJIET is committed to providing value-based quality education with professional satisfaction. It has created a pleasant, professional learning environment with state-of-the-art facilities to meet the world-class standards to provide an excellent education.
